# Data Subject Rights

## Article 15 - Access
- Authenticate requester.
- Export profile, messages, connector metadata, and audit-relevant records.
- Deliver machine-readable package within SLA.

## Article 16 - Rectification
- Validate requested correction.
- Update source-of-truth records.
- Log correction event and notify downstream processors where required.

## Article 17 - Erasure
- Verify legal basis and retention exceptions.
- Disable active processing, revoke tokens, enqueue deletion workflows.
- Confirm deletion completion and archive proof.

## Article 20 - Portability
- Generate structured export (JSON/CSV) of user-provided and observed data.
- Include schema notes and timestamped export manifest.

## Article 21 - Objection
- Register processing objection flag.
- Stop non-essential processing and profiling features.
- Confirm policy enforcement in runtime controls.

## SLA and Tracking
- Intake to acknowledgement: <= 72 hours.
- Completion target: <= 30 days unless legal extension applies.
