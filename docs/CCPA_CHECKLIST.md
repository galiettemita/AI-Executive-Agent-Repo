# CCPA/CPRA Readiness Checklist

## Consumer Rights
- Right to know what data is collected and why
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt out of sale/sharing (N/A if no sale)

## Operational Requirements
- Update privacy policy with CCPA disclosures
- Provide a deletion request workflow
- Maintain a response log for CCPA requests

## Evidence
- Track in SOC2 readiness tracker
