# RB-V92-004: Streaming SLA Breach
## Trigger
First-byte latency exceeds 500ms for more than 5% of requests.

## Detection
`BREVIO_streaming_first_byte_ms` histogram breach and `FIRST_BYTE_SLA_BREACH` warnings.

## Immediate Actions
1. Force immediate ACK mode for affected channels.
2. Check queue depth and worker saturation.
3. Disable non-critical background enrichment steps temporarily.

## Mitigation
1. Scale streaming workers and rebalance queue consumers.
2. Identify slow middleware stage in trace spans and apply timeout guards.
3. Trim payload size/chunk policy where safe.

## Recovery
1. Restore normal progressive streaming path gradually.
2. Re-enable deferred enrichers once first-byte stabilizes.
3. Record incident timeline with before/after latency percentiles.

## Verification
1. P95 first-byte <= 500ms for at least 30 minutes.
2. No elevated error rate from ACK/stream path.
3. Customer-visible typing/progress indicators behave normally.

## Escalation
Escalate to platform on-call when breach persists >15 minutes or impacts premium/critical workspaces.
