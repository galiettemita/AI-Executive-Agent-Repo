# GDPR Readiness Checklist (EU Launch Later)

Even with US-only launch, build GDPR-ready controls now.

## Lawful Basis
- Document lawful basis for each processing activity.
- Record consent for integrations.

## Data Subject Rights
- Access and portability (export endpoint).
- Right to erasure (delete endpoint).
- Rectification (update profile).

## Data Minimization
- Store only necessary data.
- Prefer metadata over full content.

## Security
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Access control and audit logs.
- Incident response plan.

## Processors and DPAs
- Maintain a list of subprocessors.
- Execute DPAs with vendors.

## International Transfers
- Prepare SCCs if EU data processed in US.

## DPIA
- Perform DPIA before EU launch or major new processing.
