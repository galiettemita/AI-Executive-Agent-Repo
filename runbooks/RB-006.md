# RB-006: Audit Log Integrity or Hash-Chain Alert

## Trigger
- Hash-chain discontinuity in `audit_log_entries`.
- Unauthorized UPDATE/DELETE attempt on append-only audit table.

## Detection
- Verify DB trigger protection fired for forbidden mutation.
- Recompute chain from last known-good anchor hash.

## Immediate Actions
1. Switch affected workflows to approval-required mode.
2. Snapshot current audit table and related WAL offsets.
3. Restrict audit-writer role and rotate credentials if needed.

## Mitigation
- Restore audit writer role with least privilege.
- Repair forward chain by appending correction records (never mutate).
- Document discrepancy and root cause in incident trail.

## Recovery
- Resume normal execution only after chain continuity is revalidated.

## Verification
- Trigger enforcement test passes.
- Chain recomputation matches stored hashes from incident point forward.

## Escalation
- Security and compliance owners must approve closure.
