# RB-007: Onboarding Workflow Stall

## Trigger
- `onboarding_v1` stage times out or repeated extraction failures.
- Discovery question completion rate drops.

## Detection
- Inspect workflow steps for stalled stage.
- Check replay-store hit/miss and extractor schema validation failures.

## Immediate Actions
1. Pause auto-advance to next onboarding stage.
2. Retry extraction with deterministic replay key.
3. Notify operator with stage and failing schema id.

## Mitigation
- Patch malformed prompt/schema mappings.
- Regenerate stage payload from fixture answers if parse failed.
- Confirm `workspace_profiles/personas/behavior_policies` write paths.

## Recovery
- Resume from stalled stage after successful dry-run.

## Verification
- All four stages complete with version_int >= 1.
- No unresolved unparsed discovery lines remain.

## Escalation
- Product + platform on-call if onboarding completion SLA is breached.
