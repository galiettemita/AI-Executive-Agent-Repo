# Responsible Disclosure

## Reporting
- Email: security@yourassistant.com
- Include steps to reproduce and impact summary

## Safe Harbor
- We welcome good‑faith research.
- Do not access data beyond what is necessary to prove the issue.
- Avoid social engineering and destructive testing.

## Response Targets
- Acknowledge within 3 business days
- Provide a fix plan within 14 days for valid reports
