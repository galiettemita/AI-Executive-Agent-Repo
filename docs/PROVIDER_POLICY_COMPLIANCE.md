# Provider Policy Compliance

## Meta/WhatsApp
- Verified business and app review
- Message template compliance
- Webhook verification and encryption

## Google / Microsoft OAuth
- Limit scopes to minimum required
- Provide user consent and revocation

## Stripe
- Follow payment and refund policies
- No storage of raw card data

## Twilio (if used)
- Consent for messaging
- Opt-out handling

## OpenAI
- Follow usage and safety policies
- Avoid storing prohibited content

## Evidence
- Store policy confirmations and approvals in SOC2 tracker
