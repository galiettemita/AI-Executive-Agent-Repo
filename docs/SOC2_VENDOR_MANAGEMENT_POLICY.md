# SOC 2 Vendor Management Policy

## Purpose
Manage third-party risk for vendors and subprocessors.

## Vendor Onboarding
- Perform a security and privacy review before use.
- Execute required DPAs or BAAs where applicable.
- Record vendor purpose, data access, and retention.

## Ongoing Management
- Annual review of critical vendors.
- Track vendor incidents and security updates.
- Replace vendors that no longer meet security requirements.

## Evidence
- Maintain a vendor inventory and review notes.
