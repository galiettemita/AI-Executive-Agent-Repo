# Privacy Policy (Framework Draft)

Legal review required before launch. This document is a structured framework aligned with US-only launch and HIPAA considerations.

## 1) Data We Collect
- Identity and account data (user id, contact, auth tokens).
- Profile and preferences (including optional location).
- Messages and communications (WhatsApp, email metadata, calendar metadata).
- Files/photos (stored in object storage; metadata in database).
- Voice transcripts/summaries (recordings retained by policy).
- Usage, billing, and analytics.

## 2) How We Use Data
- Provide and improve the assistant.
- Deliver notifications and automation.
- Security, fraud prevention, and compliance.

## 3) Health Data
- Health-related data is processed when provided by the user.
- Data is handled under HIPAA-aligned safeguards.
- For covered entities, a BAA is required for PHI.

## 4) Data Sharing
- Third-party processors for hosting, storage, payments, and messaging.
- No sale of personal data.
- Enterprise DPAs where applicable.

## 5) Data Retention
- See Data Retention Policy (DATA_RETENTION.md).
- Users can request deletion and export.

## 6) User Rights
- Access, deletion, and portability.
- Consent management for integrations.

## 7) Security
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Access controls, audit logs, and key rotation.

## 8) Children
- Service not intended for users under 13.

## 9) Changes
- Material changes will be communicated in advance.
