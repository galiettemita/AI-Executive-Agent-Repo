# Terms of Service (Framework Draft)

Legal review required before launch. This document defines the sections and baseline language needed for a production-grade ToS.

## 1) Service Description
- Executive AI assistant delivered through WhatsApp and web.
- Provides planning, communications, reminders, and automation.
- Integrations with third-party services (Google, Microsoft, Stripe, Meta, etc.).

## 2) Eligibility
- US-only launch.
- Users must be 13+ (no COPPA coverage).
- Enterprise users may have separate terms and DPA.

## 3) User Responsibilities
- Provide accurate information.
- Maintain account security.
- Do not misuse the service or attempt unauthorized access.

## 4) Health Data and Disclaimers
- The service may process health-related data.
- The assistant is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.
- For HIPAA-covered workflows, a BAA is required with enterprise customers.

## 5) Third-Party Services
- User authorizes connections to external providers.
- Provider terms apply (Meta, Google, Microsoft, Stripe, etc.).

## 6) Payments and Subscriptions
- Pricing, billing cadence, and refunds.
- Failed payments and suspension policy.

## 7) Acceptable Use
- No abuse, harassment, or illegal use.
- No attempts to reverse engineer, scrape, or overload systems.

## 8) Termination
- User can terminate at any time.
- We can suspend for abuse or non-payment.

## 9) Liability and Warranty
- Service provided "as is".
- Liability limitations and disclaimers (legal review required).

## 10) Governing Law
- United States; state specified by counsel (default: Delaware/California).

## 11) Changes to Terms
- Advance notice for material changes.
