# RB-003: Budget Exhaustion or Cost Spike

## Trigger
- Frequent `BUDGET_CALLS_EXHAUSTED` decisions.
- Sudden increase in call/cost usage outside expected profile.

## Detection
- Inspect `provisioning_monthly_usage` and rate-limit ledger events.
- Check admin cost summary and anomalies endpoints for deltas.

## Immediate Actions
1. Reduce autonomy to confirmation-required for elevated/critical tools.
2. Pause proactive actions for affected workspaces.
3. Notify operators with impacted workspace list and top cost drivers.

## Mitigation
- Increase budget only with owner/admin approval and incident note.
- Tighten per-tool caps and disable high-cost providers temporarily.
- Enable fallback models for lower-cost tiers.

## Recovery
- Resume normal policy once spend slope returns to baseline.
- Backfill trust receipts and budget decision evidence.

## Verification
- Budget denial rate stabilizes.
- No unapproved budget overrides remain active.

## Escalation
- Finance and platform on-call if spend anomaly > 2x baseline for > 30 minutes.
