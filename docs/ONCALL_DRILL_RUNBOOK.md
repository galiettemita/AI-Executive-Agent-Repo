# Incident Response + On-Call Drill

## Goal
Validate incident response and escalation procedures.

## Steps
1. Simulate a P1 incident (e.g., disable a non-critical endpoint in staging).
2. Open an incident channel and assign roles.
3. Document timeline and mitigation steps.
4. Complete a postmortem within 5 business days.
