# User Research Framework

## Personas
- Executive operator: high volume schedule, wants delegation and clarity.
- Busy professional: needs reminders, inbox triage, and quick actions.
- Caregiver/parent: coordination across family calendars and reminders.
- Founder/entrepreneur: travel, meetings, and rapid context switching.

## Use Case Scenarios
- Schedule meetings and resolve conflicts automatically.
- Summarize inbox and draft replies with approval.
- Provide wardrobe suggestions based on calendar and weather.
- Proactive reminders for travel, payments, and tasks.

## Pain Point Analysis
- Too many channels (email, calendar, WhatsApp, SMS).
- Missed deadlines due to overload.
- Manual triage of important messages.
- Disjointed tools and repetitive workflows.

## Competitive Analysis
- Virtual assistants (human): high cost, limited scalability.
- Productivity apps: fragmented, require manual input.
- Calendar/email tools: narrow focus, no cross-domain orchestration.
