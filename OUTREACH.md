# Outreach copy — 3 friends, Thursday

Read the design doc's instruction first: this is not a pitch, not a feature list, not a "I'd love to get your thoughts" softball. It's a one-paragraph message that gets a yes/no. The response (or non-response) tells you more than market research.

## The format

- iMessage / SMS / WhatsApp — whatever you already text them on
- ONE paragraph
- No links, no screenshots, no Loom, no deck
- End with a yes/no ask
- Send all three in the same hour so you can compare reactions

---

## Draft A — for a friend whose inbox FOMO you've literally witnessed

Hey — I'm building a thing that watches my inbox and only texts me when something looks like I'd actually be sad to miss it. Quiet 99% of the time, right when it speaks. I think you have the same problem I do (remember [insert specific moment they vented about email]?). Want to try it when it's ready in ~2 weeks?

## Draft B — for a friend you respect but haven't witnessed the pain on

Working on something new. It's an AI that watches your Gmail and only texts you about emails you'd genuinely regret missing — like 1-3 a day max, not a digest. I'd want your honest read on whether it actually saves time or just becomes one more notification. Want in when it's ready in ~2 weeks?

## Draft C — for a founder/consultant friend who lives in their inbox

I'm building a personal AI that reads your inbox and only texts you about the stuff that matters — investor replies, hot leads, RSVPs, the email you've been waiting on. Cap of 3 alerts a day, no exceptions. Trying to find 3-5 people to be brutal beta testers. You in?

---

## What to do with the responses

**Yes** → reply "great, no setup yet, I'll text you in ~2 weeks." Add a row to FRIENDS.md.

**Yes-but-when?** → "honest answer 2-3 weeks, building this week, want you in the first batch."

**Maybe / probably** → treat as no. They'll be polite when you ask later too. Save them for v1.0+.

**No response** → no follow-up. Real demand replies same-day.

**Asks what stack you're using / wants to invest / wants to build it with you** → not who you're looking for right now. Park them.

## After all three reply (or fail to reply)

Write a one-line note at the bottom of this file: `0/3` or `2/3` or `3/3`. That number is your first real demand signal. If it's `0/3`, do not start week 2 — go back to the wedge.
