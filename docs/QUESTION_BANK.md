# Question Bank

## Basics
- What name should I use?
- What timezone are you in?
- What are your top three priorities this month?

## Work & Goals
- What role and company should I use in updates?
- What meetings are most important to protect?
- What KPIs or metrics do you track weekly?

## Scheduling & Preferences
- What times are off-limits for meetings?
- Do you prefer short summaries or detailed reports?
- How should I handle last-minute changes?

## Communication
- Which channel should I use for urgent items?
- Who should I copy on key updates?
- Are there topics I should avoid sharing proactively?

## Travel & Logistics
- Do you prefer morning or evening flights?
- What seat, airline, or hotel preferences do you have?
- What is your default budget range for travel?

## Personalization & Style
- What tone should I use in messages to you?
- Are there templates or formats you want me to follow?
- What recurring tasks should I turn into automation?

## Delegation
- Who can I delegate to, and what are their roles?
- What should be escalated immediately vs. batched?
- What level of detail do you want in delegation updates?

## Privacy & Data
- Are there data types you do not want stored?
- Do you want to opt in to anonymized insights sharing?
- How long should I retain task history?
